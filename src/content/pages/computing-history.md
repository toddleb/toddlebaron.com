# A Journey Through Computing

## Mainframes (1970s-80s)
The big iron era. Green screens and JCL job cards.

## Symbolics Lisp Machine (1980s)
Genera OS. The most exotic machine I ever touched.

## Apple IIe (1983)
Green phosphor CRT. Applesoft BASIC. That `]` prompt.

## BBS / Dial-Up (Late 80s-90s)
ANSI art and 14.4k modems.

## Sun SPARCstation / OpenLook (Early 90s)
Those rounded pushpins and distinctive scrollbars.

## SGI IRIX (Mid 90s)
The purple-teal Indigo Magic desktop. The coolest workstation ever made.

## NeXTSTEP (Late 80s-Early 90s)
Dark gray panels. Where the web was born.

## BeOS (Late 90s)
Yellow tab bars. Ahead of its time.
